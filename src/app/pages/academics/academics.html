Class 1 to 10 Syllabus

At Sri Mahatma Public School, our curriculum for Class 1 to 10 is designed to challenge students intellectually while providing hands-on learning experiences. We prepare our students for future success with an adaptive and progressive syllabus.

  • auto_stories Primary (Class 1-5): Foundational literacy, numeracy, environmental science, and interactive storytelling.
  • science Middle (Class 6-8): Introduction to advanced sciences, mathematics, robotics basics, and coding fundamentals.
  • rocket_launch High School (Class 9-10): Board exam prep, advanced STEM subjects, literature, and career counseling.
  • language Future Skills: English proficiency, digital literacy, and logic-building integration.
